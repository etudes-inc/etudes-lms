ETUDES-NG is used in hybrid, online, blended, and traditional courses and for collaboration projects. The same features are often used in different ways by instructors to meet a teaching goal or style. ETUDES-NG offers a variety of features for use in courses, including:

My Workspace

for a personalized view of information from your sites and your preferences

Schedule

for posting and viewing deadlines, events, etc

Announcements

for posting current, time-critical information

Syllabus

for c reating a course syllabus

Modules

for publishing and viewing content, i.e. lessons, modules, units

Assignments

for posting, submitting, and grading online

Discussion & Private Messages

for participating in asynchronous discussion, group work, and collaboration, and private communication amongst students and instructors

Chat

for real-time conversation in written form

Email Archive

for viewing email sent to the site's group email address

Tests & Quizzes

for authoring and administering exams, quizzes, surveys, etc.

Gradebook

for posting and managing grades of registered students

Resources

for posting documents and URLs

News/RSS

for viewing content from online sources (RSS feeds)

Web Content

for accessing an external website within the site
